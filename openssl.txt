To make Asymmetric encryption & decryption use these commands:
openssl genpkey -algorithm RSA -out private_key.pem -pkeyopt rsa_keygen_bits:2048
openssl rsa -pubout -in private_key.pem -out public_key.pem
openssl rsautl -encrypt -inkey public_key.pem -pubin -in myfile.txt -out myfile.enc
openssl rsautl -decrypt -inkey private_key.pem -in myfile.enc -out myfile_decrypted.txt

if you have your public & private keys and want To make a signature:
openssl dgst -sha256 -sign private_key.pem -out signature.bin myfile.txt
Verify the signature using the public key:
openssl dgst -sha256 -verify public_key.pem -signature signature.bin myfile.txt
